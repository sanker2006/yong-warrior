# BMAD Brief

## Product Goal

Build a mobile-first training terminal for Ningbo Yongshi where a learner can register, study the course map, complete question sets, and produce a real ability profile. Administrators only need a read-only view of users, progress, and question performance.

## Roles

- Learner: registers, studies courses, answers questions, reviews profile.
- Administrator: views registered users, progress, question attempts, and skill-domain status.

## Main Loop

1. Register or log in.
2. Continue today's recommended course.
3. Complete course week.
4. Enter question bank by quick set, course week, or knowledge domain.
5. Submit answers and save attempts.
6. Generate ability profile from real records.
7. Administrator reviews user progress and question data.

## Not Now

Instructor operations, equipment and venue management, AAR workflow, achievements surface, complex question-bank games, and write operations in admin are outside Sprint 1.5 and Sprint 2.

## Skill Domains

Shooting basics, squad coordination, CQB and building search, tactical movement, SOP flow, communications command, integrated opposition, leadership and psychological quality.
